University Level

We implore the University of Michigan to use more of their endowment to make up for the lack of federal financial aide that undocumented students may not receive

State Level

We implore the State of Michigan to create laws that grant in state residency status to undocumented students who have lived in Michigan for 36 months before completing their high school diploma or GED such as states like Texas have

Federal Level

We implore the United States of America to codify Plyer V. Doe and extend the protections through higher education in public institutions
